## PROJECT OVERVIEW

Build a premium, evidence-based personal portfolio website for Dr. Peter Lewinski at **peterlewinski.com** (or similar domain). The site serves as a living proof-of-work document: every major claim links to verifiable evidence (diploma scans, Google Scholar, press articles, app screenshots, photos).

**Aesthetic Direction:** Luxury editorial meets dark-mode tech. Think: Audemars Piguet website crossed with a Bloomberg Terminal. Matte black backgrounds, gold/champagne accents, serif headlines (e.g., Playfair Display or similar), clean sans-serif body text. Full-bleed hero images. Parallax scroll. Smooth section transitions. No generic AI slop.

**Tech Stack:** Static site (Next.js or Astro preferred for performance + SEO). Tailwind CSS. Framer Motion or GSAP for animations. Deployed on Vercel or Cloudflare Pages.

**Mobile-first.** Must look stunning on iPhone 15 Pro Max.
